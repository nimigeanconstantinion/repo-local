RULARE LOCALA (ex: minikube/docker-desktop)

1) Intra in proiect:
cd /Users/bogdandaniel/Documents/proiecte-elevi/contantin-helm-external-libraries

2) Actualizeaza dependintele:
helm dependency update

3) Verifica chart-ul:
helm lint .

4) Vezi manifestele generate:
helm template resurse-testing-app . -f values.yaml -f values-bgd.yaml

5) Deploy local:
helm upgrade --install resurse-testing-app . -n <namespace> -f values.yaml -f values-bgd.yaml
