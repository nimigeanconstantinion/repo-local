RULARE PE ENV (dev/staging/prod)

1) Intra in proiect:
cd /Users/bogdandaniel/Documents/proiecte-elevi/contantin-helm-external-libraries

2) Alege fisierul de env:
- dev: values-dev.yaml
- staging: values-staging.yaml
- prod: values-prod.yaml
- bgd: values-bgd.yaml

3) Actualizeaza dependintele:
helm dependency update

4) Verifica chart-ul:
helm lint .

5) Vezi manifestele:
helm template resurse-testing-app . -f values.yaml -f values-<env>.yaml

6) Deploy:
helm upgrade --install resurse-testing-app . -n <namespace> -f values.yaml -f values-<env>.yaml
